PopUp Collapsible Table

Worked on a team of three mechanical engineers to design and develop a table which allows the user to easily and in one fluid motion remove it from the furniture-scape of the room. The PopUp Table synthesizes spatial transition technologies adapted from pop-up books with material research to create a light-weight and highly functional furniture essential.

Angle Relationship Derivation

Began by deriving equations detailing he relationships between the three defining angles of a strut. This aided the design downselection process.

Finite Element Analysis

Conducted multiple rounds of Finite Element Analysis to compare different designs.

Composite Material Development

Developed composite material to combine the low density of cardboard and the strength of epoxy while keeping cost down.

Composite Material Testing

Conducted Creep and Bending tests on new material to determine material properties
