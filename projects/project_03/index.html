Stochastic Motion Energy Harvester

This patent pending design allows for energy harvesting from non uniform inputs. I tested variations on design to optimize generator configuration. The housing and generator were designed to avoid interference with the PCB. Design iterated and adjusted for injection molding and assembly. The embedded video shows the invention used as an energy harvesting dog collar for marketing purposes. Worked with a team of Electrical Engineers to ensure compatibility with embedded PCB.
